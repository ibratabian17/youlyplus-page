Immersive Lyrics for YouTube Music

Tired of static, plain text? YouLy+ transforms your listening session into a rich, interactive, and beautifully animated karaoke-style experience directly inside YouTube Music.

Add to Your Browser

Why You'll Love YouLy+

Zero Configuration

Install and it just works. YouLy+ activates automatically on YouTube Music. No setup, no API keys neededâ€”just play a song and enjoy. (except for Gemini AI)

Karaoke-Style Sync

Experience perfectly timed lyrics with line-by-line and even word-by-word highlighting for a true, immersive sing-along session.

Animated Backgrounds

Enjoy a stunning, Apple Music-style animated blur of the album art, creating a beautiful and dynamic player view that brings music to life.

Advanced Translation

Instantly translate lyrics with Google Translate, or unlock powerful AI translations with Gemini. Includes Romanization for J-Pop/K-Pop. (For some songs, romanization are handled by backend, not gemini/gtranslate)

Native Feels Like!

We make the extension so lightweight! we also have some features like click-to-seek, multi-line active, in-app enable romanization/translation!

SponsorBlock Integration

Automatically fix the lyrics to add delay on non-music segments like intros, outros, and sponsor messages for an uninterrupted listening session.
