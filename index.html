Features that hit different

We didn't just add lyrics. We rebuilt the entire visual experience to match the vibe of your favorite tracks.

It Just Works.

Zero setup required. Install the extension, open YouTube Music, and play a song. YouLy+ automatically handles the rest. No mandatory API keys or login walls required to get started.

Precision Sync

Experience lyrics that flow perfectly in time with the beat. We support studio-grade line-by-line and word-by-word syncing (Karaoke style) for maximum immersion.

Dynamic Visuals

Say goodbye to static backgrounds. YouLy+ pulls colors from the album art to create a living, breathing backdrop that adapts to every track you play.

Polyglot Power

Listening to K-Pop or J-Rock? Get instant, official Romanization directly from our database, or use Gemini AI & Google Translate for context-aware translations.

SponsorBlock Integration

We know you skip the intros. YouLy+ intelligently detects non-music segments (like intros or music video dialogue) so your lyrics never drift out of sync.
