Privacy Policy for YouLy+

Effective Date: March 7, 2026
Last Updated: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction and Scope

Welcome to YouLy+ ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy governs your use of the YouLy+ browser extension ("the Extension") developed by Ibra Al Tabian. By installing and using the Extension, you agree to the information practices described in this document.

Our core philosophy is absolute data minimization and transparency. We believe that your music listening habits are your own business. Therefore, YouLy+ is engineered from the ground up to operate without collecting, storing, or monetizing your personal data.

This policy explains in detail what data is processed, how our architecture protects your privacy, and your rights as a user.

2. Definitions

  • "Extension" refers to the YouLy+ browser add-on available for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and other Chromium-based browsers.
  • "Personal Data" refers to any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (e.g., name, email, IP address).
  • "Ephemeral Data" refers to data that is processed temporarily in computer memory (RAM) and is immediately destroyed once a specific task is completed, without being written to a disk or database.

3. Information We Strictly Do NOT Collect

To be entirely clear, YouLy+ operates without tracking you. We do not collect, record, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII): We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, physical address, or phone number.
  • Account Credentials: We do not have access to your YouTube, Google, Apple Music, or Tidal passwords or account details.
  • IP Addresses: We do not log the IP addresses from which you access the Extension or our serverless API.
  • Browsing History: The Extension only activates on explicitly whitelisted music domains (e.g., music.youtube.com, music.apple.com, listen.tidal.com). It cannot and does not monitor, read, or transmit your activity on any other websites.
  • Telemetry and Analytics: We do not use hidden analytics trackers (such as Google Analytics or Mixpanel) to monitor feature usage, click rates, or session lengths.

4. How Your Data is Processed (Zero-Storage Architecture)

To provide real-time, synchronized lyrics and translations, the Extension must process current song metadata (such as the track title, artist name, and album). This data processing is strictly functional and ephemeral.

A. The KPoe API (Serverless Backend)

When the Extension cannot find lyrics locally, it requests them via our custom backend, the KPoe API.

  • Stateless Execution: The KPoe API is built on a serverless architecture. This means it spins up to execute a single search request and spins down immediately after.
  • Zero Logging: The API acts purely as a real-time proxy and pass-through. It does not contain a database, and it does not write logs of incoming requests, song searches, or user activity. Once the lyrics are delivered to your browser, all memory of the transaction is permanently erased.

B. SponsorBlock Integration and K-Anonymity

To skip non-music segments in music videos, the Extension queries the crowdsourced SponsorBlock API.

  • Privacy by Design: To prevent third parties from knowing exactly what video you are watching, YouLy+ utilizes hashed video IDs. The Extension mathematically scrambles the video ID and only sends a partial prefix (the first few characters of the hash) to the API.
  • Result: This creates a privacy shield where the API returns a batch of possible segment skips, and the final filtering is done locally on your machine.

C. Translation and Romanization Services

If you explicitly activate live translation or Romanization features, the raw lyric text is sent to third-party translation providers (such as Google Translate or Gemini AI). Only the lyric text is transmitted; no user identifiers are attached to these translation requests.

5. Local Storage, Caching, and Required Permissions

To run efficiently and minimize network traffic, YouLy+ leverages your browser’s isolated local storage environments (such as IndexedDB).

Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Cached Lyrics: Once lyrics are downloaded, they are saved locally to your hard drive. If you replay the song, the Extension reads the local file rather than reaching out to the internet.
  • User Preferences: Your UI settings, theme choices, and sync offsets are saved directly within your browser profile.
  • Custom Lyric Files: If you upload your own .lrc or .json files, they remain strictly confined to your local device.

Browser Permissions Explained:

To achieve this, the Extension asks for the following standard permissions during installation:

  • host permissions (e.g., *://music.youtube.com/*): Required to "read" the DOM to identify the currently playing track and to "write" to the DOM to inject the dynamic YouLy+ graphical interface over the default player.
  • storage: Required to read and write your cached lyrics and settings to your local browser profile.

6. Third-Party Services

While our infrastructure retains zero data, fetching lyrics and skipping segments relies on open APIs. Your use of YouLy+ means anonymous, ephemeral metadata may be processed by the following third-party services, subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • LRCLIB (Open-source lyric database)
  • SponsorBlock (Crowdsourced segment skipping)
  • Google Translate / Gemini AI (Live translation features)

7. Data Security

Even though we do not store personal data, we take security seriously. All network requests made by the Extension to our KPoe API and third-party services are encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS protocols to prevent "man-in-the-middle" interceptions.

8. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and VCDPA)

Under global privacy laws like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), users have the right to request access to, deletion of, or portability of their personal data.

Because YouLy+ does not collect, store, or link any data to you, there is no personal data for us to export, delete, or modify upon request. You have complete control over the data generated by the Extension. If you wish to delete your locally cached lyrics and settings, you can do so at any time by clearing your browser's extension data or uninstalling YouLy+.

9. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no personal data, no data from minors is stored on our systems.

10. Open Source Transparency

We believe trust is earned through transparency. YouLy+ is fully open-source. Security researchers, developers, and users are encouraged to audit our codebase at any time to verify that our privacy practices match our code.

  • Source Code Repository: https://github.com/ibratabian17/YouLyPlus

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect structural updates, new features, or legal requirements. If substantial changes are made regarding how data is handled, we will indicate the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document and provide a notice within the Extension's GitHub repository.
